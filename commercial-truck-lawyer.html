Frequently Asked Questions

How can an 18-wheeler accident attorney help me after a collision?

An 18-wheeler accident attorney can provide legal guidance and representation to ensure your rights are protected. They can investigate the accident, gather evidence, negotiate with insurance companies, and pursue compensation for damages such as medical expenses, lost wages, pain and suffering, and property damage. Additionally, they can advocate for you in court if necessary.

What damages can I recover with the help of an 18-wheeler accident attorney?

With the assistance of an 18-wheeler accident attorney, you may be able to recover various damages, including medical expenses, lost wages, property damage, pain and suffering, emotional distress, loss of consortium, and in some cases, punitive damages.

What steps should I take to protect my rights after an 18-wheeler accident?

To protect your rights after an 18-wheeler accident, document the scene, seek medical attention, notify law enforcement and your insurance company, avoid discussing fault or signing any documents without legal guidance, and consult with an experienced attorney as soon as possible.
