Can I still pursue compensation if I was partially at fault for the 18-wheeler accident?

Depending on the laws of the state where the accident occurred, you may still be able to pursue compensation even if you were partially at fault for the 18-wheeler accident. Some states follow comparative negligence rules, where your compensation may be reduced based on your degree of fault.
